Sci-Fi and Fantasy

Dune – Frank Herbert

Question: What is the name of the secretive sisterhood in Dune that possesses advanced mental and physical abilities?
Answer: Bene Gesserit.
Question: What is the term used for the giant sandworms native to the desert planet Arrakis in Dune?
Answer: Shai-Hulud.
Question: What is the name of the Fremen garment that conserves water?
Answer: Stillsuit.
Question: What is a muad’dib?
Answer: Desert mouse.
Question: What is the last thing Paul must do before he is accepted as a full Fremen?
Answer: Ride a sandworm.

The Lord of the Rings – J.R.R. Tolkien

Question: In The Lord of the Rings, what is the name of the inn where Frodo and his companions meet Aragorn in Bree?
Answer: The Prancing Pony.
Question: How many Rings of Power were given to the Dwarf-lords according to the inscription in The Lord of the Rings?
Answer: Seven.
Question: How many members of the fellowship are there?
Answer: Nine.
Question: Which two members of the fellowship are cousins?
Answer: Merry and Pippin.

A Game of Thrones – George R.R. Martin

Question: In A Game of Thrones, what is the ancestral seat of House Stark?
Answer: Winterfell.
Question: What is the motto of House Lannister in A Game of Thrones?
Answer: "Hear Me Roar!"
Question: In A Game of Thrones, what is the name of Jon Snow's direwolf, and what does it symbolize about his character?
Answer: Ghost. It symbolizes Jon’s reserved, observant, and solitary nature, as well as his unique role within the Stark family.
Question: Which historical conflict inspired the political intrigue and wars depicted in A Game of Thrones?
Answer: The Wars of the Roses, a series of civil wars in England during the 15th century between the houses of Lancaster and York.

Ender's Game – Orson Scott Card

Question: In Ender's Game, what is the name of the military school where Ender Wiggin is trained to fight the alien species?
Answer: Battle School.
Question: What is the pejorative term used for the alien species in Ender's Game?
Answer: Buggers.

The Left Hand of Darkness – Ursula K. Le Guin

Question: On which planet does the story of The Left Hand of Darkness take place?
Answer: Gethen.

The Hobbit – J.R.R. Tolkien

Question: In The Hobbit, what is the name of the dragon that occupies the Lonely Mountain?
Answer: Smaug.
Question: What is the name of Bilbo Baggins' home village in The Hobbit?
Answer: Hobbiton.

Hyperion – Dan Simmons

Question: In Hyperion, what is the name of the mysterious creature associated with the Time Tombs that the pilgrims seek?
Answer: The Shrike.
