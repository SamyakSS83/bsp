Gone Girl – Gillian Flynn

Question: What is the name of the bar that Nick Dunne owns with his sister Margo in Gone Girl?
Answer: The bar is called The Bar.

The Da Vinci Code – Dan Brown

Question: Which secret society is central to the plot of The Da Vinci Code?
Answer: The Priory of Sion.
Question: What is the name of the albino monk assassin in the novel?
Answer: Silas.

Big Little Lies – Liane Moriarty

Question: What is the name of the middle school that the children attend in Big Little Lies?
Answer: Pirriwee Public School.
Question: Which character is revealed to have an abusive relationship with her husband, Perry?
Answer: Celeste Wright.

Murder on the Orient Express – Agatha Christie

Question: What is the real name of the murder victim, Ratchett, in Murder on the Orient Express?
Answer: His real name is Cassetti.
Question: Which famous detective is the protagonist of the novel?
Answer: Hercule Poirot.

The Hound of the Baskervilles – Arthur Conan Doyle

Question: What is the name of the Baskerville family estate in the novel?
Answer: Baskerville Hall.
Question: Who is the naturalist neighbor and secret antagonist in the story?
Answer: Jack Stapleton.
