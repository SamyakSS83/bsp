Modern Literature

The Road – Cormac McCarthy

Question: What food do the father and son discover in a hidden bunker that becomes a rare moment of hope?
Answer: Canned goods and fresh water.
Question: What recurring phrase does the father use to reassure the son about their morality?
Answer: "We’re carrying the fire."

Life of Pi – Yann Martel

Question: Piscine Molitor Patel is named after?
Answer: A swimming pool, Piscina Marabilis.
Question: What is the name of the tiger?
Answer: Richard Parker.

The Goldfinch – Donna Tartt

Question: What is the name of the painting Theo steals during the museum bombing?
Answer: The Goldfinch by Carel Fabritius.
Question: In which European city does Theo reunite with Boris years after their teenage friendship?
Answer: Amsterdam.

Normal People – Sally Rooney

Question: Which character says: ‘Everyone likes you. And unlike most people you’re actually a nice person.’
Answer: Marianne.
Question: Where do Connell and Marianne first see each other at university?
Answer: At a party.

Circe – Madeline Miller

Question: What gift does Helios give Circe as a punishment for her defiance?
Answer: He banishes her to the island of Aiaia.
Question: Who is the first mortal to visit Circe’s island and teach her the consequences of her powers?
Answer: Glaucus.

A Man Called Ove – Fredrik Backman

Question: What car did Rune purchase that Ove claims is the reason for their ended friendship?
Answer: BMW.
Question: What did Tom steal from Ove?
Answer: His father’s watch.

The Midnight Library – Matt Haig

Question: What is the name of the librarian who guides Nora in the Midnight Library?
Answer: Mrs. Elm.
Question: What is the regret tied to the alternate life where Nora becomes a glaciologist?
Answer: She dies in an avalanche.

Where the Crawdads Sing – Delia Owens

Question: What nickname does Kya earn from the townspeople due to her reclusive nature?
Answer: The Marsh Girl.
Question: What bird does Kya closely associate with her late mother?
Answer: A heron.

The Night Circus – Erin Morgenstern

Question: What is the name of the magical clock created by Herr Thiessen for the circus?
Answer: The Wunderschön Clock.
Question: What color scheme is strictly followed by performers and attendees of the circus?
Answer: Black, white, and shades of grey.
