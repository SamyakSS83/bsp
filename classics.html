Pride and Prejudice – Jane Austen

Answer: Wishbone.
Question: Which 1995 miniseries adaptation of Pride and Prejudice, starring Colin Firth as Mr. Darcy, is considered a benchmark for future adaptations?
Answer: The BBC miniseries Pride and Prejudice.

To Kill a Mockingbird – Harper Lee

Answer: Gregory Peck.

Answer: Mary Badham.
