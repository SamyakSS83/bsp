Young Adult

The Hunger Games – Suzanne Collins

Question: What is the significance of the plant katniss in the book, aside from being the protagonist’s name?
Answer: It's an edible plant symbolizing survival, much like Katniss's role as a survivor.
Question: In which District does Gale work in the mines, and what resource does it produce?
Answer: District 12, which produces coal.

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer’s Stone – J.K. Rowling

Question: What is the first spell Hermione Granger successfully demonstrates to Harry and Ron?
Answer: Reparo.
Question: What is the date that Harry receives his first chocolate frog?
Answer: 1 Sept, 1991.

The Fault in Our Stars – John Green

Question: What is the title of the fictional book that Hazel and Augustus obsess over?
Answer: An Imperial Affliction.

Eleanor & Park – Rainbow Rowell

Question: What comic book series does Park introduce to Eleanor on the school bus?
Answer: Watchmen.
Question: What color is Eleanor’s iconic hair, often mentioned throughout the book?
Answer: Bright red.

Divergent – Veronica Roth

Answer: Being attacked by birds.
Question: Which Faction does Christina originally belong to before transferring to Dauntless?
Answer: Candor.

The Perks of Being a Wallflower – Stephen Chbosky
